# Battlecard: Example Fintech Competitor

## Competitor overview
- API-first payments platform with a strong SMB and enterprise presence [S1]

## Positioning
- Emphasizes faster integrations and global payment method coverage [S1] [S2]

## Pricing posture
- Publicly documented transaction-based pricing with custom enterprise pricing tiers [S3]

## Recent launches / announcements
- Announced expanded AI risk controls for payment fraud and dispute detection [S4]

## Strengths
- Strong developer experience and mature checkout/payment APIs [S1] [S2]

## Weaknesses / risks
- Limited publicly available detail on exact enterprise contract terms and SLAs [S3]

## Customer sentiment
- Positive feedback around API quality, mixed feedback around support response times [S5]

## Sales talk track / objection handling
- Lead with implementation speed, then pressure-test total cost, support model, and regulatory fit by segment [S1] [S3] [S5]
